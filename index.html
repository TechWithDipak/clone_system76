MEET POP!_OS

WHY POP!_OS?

Workflow

Pop!_OS is designed for fast navigation, easy window organization, and fluid, intuitive workflow. Your operating system should encourage discovery, not obstruct it.

Compatibility

Develop software out of the box with an array of libraries pre-installed, or download Pop!_OS's vast and reliable package management, making it easy to install, remove, and update all software on the OS.

Keep Your Computer Secure

Feel safe and secure with more full-disk encryption standard, keeping your data safe from unauthorized access. You're in control of system updates, installing them on your schedule to minimize disruptions. Privacy features help you to manage personal information, while firmware management ensures your hardware stays up to date with the latest security patches.

Customization

Get comfy with an extensive, easy, loaded full of preferences. Set dark, light, or mixed mode, adjust scaling, super key behavior, keyboard layout and shortcuts, privacy controls, and more. Your computer, your tool, your way, Pop!_OS.

OTHER FEATURES

Hybrid Graphics

Get maximum performance or long battery life. Toggle between NVIDIA or integrated graphics, or launch in this task-specific mode.

Gaming

Enjoy a smooth, consistent, and immersive gaming experience with excellent performance, out-of-the-box game-ready drivers, and Steam pre-installed.

Do Not Disturb

Get focused and eliminate distractions. Toggle Do Not Disturb to silence notifications and keep your workflow going.
